How Dispute Pack Builder Works

Dispute Pack Builder converts fragmented business documents into a structured, evidence-based workflow enabling SMEs to resolve disputes faster, with clarity and audit-ready outputs.

01

Evidence Collection & Structuring

Upload invoices, purchase orders, contracts, delivery notes, and emails into a unified system. The platform organises and standardises all documents into a structured dispute case environment.

02

Dispute Classification & Root Cause Mapping

Each dispute is categorised using a predefined taxonomy such as pricing issues, delivery gaps, or missing documentation automatically guiding required evidence and workflow steps.

03

Timeline Reconstruction & Evidence Linking

The system builds a chronological timeline by linking documents to events and claims, creating a clear “single version of facts” while highlighting missing or conflicting evidence.

04

Resolution Pack & Decision Support

Generate a structured, audit-ready dispute pack with evidence index, summary, and commercial resolution options enabling faster negotiation and improved payment recovery outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

◆

Understand how Dispute Pack Builder helps UK SMEs resolve invoice disputes, structure evidence, and improve cash flow through audit-ready workflows.

What is Dispute Pack Builder?

Dispute Pack Builder is a B2B invoice dispute resolution platform that helps SMEs organise fragmented documents into structured, evidence-based workflows. It creates a clear “single version of facts” to enable faster and more transparent dispute resolution.

What problem does it solve?

Businesses often manage disputes using emails, spreadsheets, and incomplete records, leading to delays and payment issues. The platform standardises dispute handling into repeatable workflows, reducing time, errors, and operational inefficiencies.

How does the platform work?

It captures invoices, contracts, emails, and delivery data, then links them into a structured evidence model (claim → event → document). A timeline is generated to reconstruct events and produce a professional dispute resolution package.

Who is it designed for?

The platform is designed for UK SMEs, finance teams, accountants, and operations professionals who regularly deal with invoice disputes and need structured, audit-ready documentation workflows.

How does it improve cash flow?

By resolving disputes faster and reducing ambiguity, businesses can recover payments more efficiently. It also prevents recurring disputes by identifying root causes and improving internal processes.

Does it provide legal advice?

No. The platform focuses on structured documentation and evidence organisation. It provides audit-ready dispute packages but does not offer legal advice, ensuring compliance and safe usage for SMEs.
